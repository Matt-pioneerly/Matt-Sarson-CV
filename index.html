    How I work

    I operate at the intersection of customer, product and growth, helping startups define their position, build the right systems, and drive results that compound.

    Increasingly that build is AI-native. I design AI-powered growth systems and orchestrate agents to carry the repetitive load: research and enrichment, content production, lifecycle and reporting. It lets a small team operate like a much larger one, which is exactly what an early-stage company needs from its first marketer.

    The rest of the full stack still applies: rebrands and category shifts, GTM launches, demand and ABM programmes, product marketing, and the teams to run it all. Before marketing I spent my early career in the charity sector and international development, which keeps pulling me back to purpose-driven work. I'm a builder by instinct, most useful when I can set the strategy and then actually build the thing.

    • Work that flows: teams that move fast but think deeply.
    • Small teams with outsized leverage, agents doing the repetitive work.
    • Brand and performance informing each other, not competing.
    • Marketing treated as a product in its own right.
    • Systems that outlast the campaign that created them.
